ABOUT

Kenneth An is a singer-songwriter and producer based in New York City. An idolizer of music his whole life, as he competed in piano competitions and have been writing songs since he was a freshman in high school, Kenneth An is now tackling his musical creativity first-hand by writing, producing, mixing, and mastering all his tracks, all with little budget. He may be labeled an amateur musician, but he has been playing music for his entire life. Although he attends Williams College, he will never let academics get in the way of pursuing his love of writing and performing music.
